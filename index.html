Benefits of ReLink

Why your company should use such a tool?

Decreases Missing Links

ReLink's ML algorithm detects the missing links between PRs and issues in your projects, and allows you to relink these missing links manually.

Improves Traceability

By removing the missing links, ReLink improves traceability in your projects, which help other software analytics tools work more efficiently.

Visualizations

In the "Project Analysis" and "Historical Graph" screens, you can see detailed graphs about your project's linking history.
